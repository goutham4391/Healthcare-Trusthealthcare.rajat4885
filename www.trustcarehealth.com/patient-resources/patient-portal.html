Having Problems Logging In?

If you are unable to login to the patient portal, please email us your name, date of birth, and phone number so we can look into the issue; you may also use the chat feature in the bottom right corner of the website.

TrustCare Health Patient Portal

We are pleased you have chosen to use the patient portal to communicate in a secure and confidential manner with your care team.

Taking just a few minutes to register will give you access to valuable information and services provided in a secure and confidential matter. Once registered and logged in, you will be able to:

  • Review and pay billing statements.
  • Research health topics.
  • Review personal health information.
  • Complete and update medical forms.
  • Update your profile and contact information.
