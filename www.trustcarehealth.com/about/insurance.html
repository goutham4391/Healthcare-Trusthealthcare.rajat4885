What We Accept

TrustCare is dedicated to making sure you have a great experience, and we want the billing process to be easy and convenient. We accept most major insurance plans. Please keep in mind that not all medical providers are in network with every insurance. Please check with your insurance company before your visit, or call the clinic directly.

We currently accept the following insurances:
  • Aetna
  • Blue Cross Blue Shield
  • CareCredit
  • Cigna
  • First Choice
  • Harmony Health
  • Humana
  • Medicaid (Laurel Only)
  • Medicare
  • Tricare
  • United Healthcare
  • Wellcare

Now Accepting CareCredit.


Complete an online CareCredit Application.

Please note that we are in network and accepting children with the following insurance plans at our TrustCare Kids location only:

  • Molina Health Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP)
  • United Healthcare Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP)
We are currently not in network for the following insurances:
(*please note that Medicaid is accepted at our Laurel location only while CHIPS is accepted at Laurel and TrustCare Kids)
  • Ambetter
  • CHIPS*
  • Magnolia*
  • MS CAN*


  • Medicaid*
  • UHC Dual Complete
  • UHC Community Plan
