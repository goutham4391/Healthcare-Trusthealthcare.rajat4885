COVID-19 Testing

Get tested for COVID-19 at any of our urgent care clinics (via walk-in only). Rapid swab tests and antibody blood tests are available.

Learn About COVID-19 Testing

COVID-19 Vaccines

Eligible Mississippi and Alabama residents may schedule an appointment online to receive a COVID-19 vaccine. Different vaccines are available at different locations. Supply is limited.

View Locations and Learn More

Patient Portal

Results from COVID-19 testing are uploaded to and accessed via the patient portal, providing communication in a secure and confidential manner with just one email and password.
